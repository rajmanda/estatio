# Payment & Billing Terms

**Estatio Inc.**
**Effective Date:** March 29, 2026

---

## 1. Subscription Plans

1.1 Estatio offers tiered subscription plans. Current plan details, pricing, and features are available at **estatio.app/pricing**.

1.2 All prices are listed in US Dollars (USD) and are exclusive of applicable taxes unless otherwise stated.

1.3 Subscription plans are billed on a monthly or annual basis as selected at checkout.

## 2. Payment Methods

2.1 We accept payment via major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover) and ACH bank transfers for annual plans.

2.2 By providing payment information, you represent that you are authorized to use the payment method and authorize Estatio to charge the applicable fees.

2.3 Payment is processed through our payment processor (Stripe, Inc.). Your payment details are transmitted securely and are not stored on Estatio servers.

## 3. Billing Cycle

3.1 **Monthly Plans:** Billing occurs on the same calendar day each month. For example, if you subscribe on March 15, subsequent billing occurs on April 15, May 15, etc.

3.2 **Annual Plans:** Billing occurs annually on the subscription start date.

3.3 If the billing date falls on a day that does not exist in a given month (e.g., the 31st), billing occurs on the last day of that month.

## 4. Automatic Renewal

4.1 Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing period unless cancelled. By subscribing, you authorize us to charge your payment method at the applicable renewal rate.

4.2 You will receive a renewal reminder email at least 7 days before the renewal date for annual subscriptions.

4.3 To cancel auto-renewal, visit your Account Settings or contact **billing@estatio.app** at least 2 business days before the renewal date.

## 5. Price Changes

5.1 We reserve the right to modify subscription fees at any time.

5.2 For existing subscribers, we will provide at least 30 days' advance notice of price increases via email.

5.3 Continued use of the Service after the effective date of a price change constitutes your acceptance of the new pricing.

5.4 Annual subscribers will not face mid-term price increases; new pricing applies at the next renewal.

## 6. Invoicing

6.1 A receipt is emailed to your registered email address upon each successful payment.

6.2 Invoices are accessible in your Account Settings under Billing.

6.3 You may request a formal invoice for accounting purposes by contacting **billing@estatio.app**.

## 7. Taxes

7.1 You are responsible for all applicable sales, use, VAT, GST, and other taxes arising from your subscription. Estatio may add applicable taxes to your invoice as required by law.

7.2 If you are exempt from sales tax, you must provide a valid exemption certificate to **billing@estatio.app** prior to billing.

## 8. Failed Payments

8.1 If a payment fails, we will retry the charge up to three times over 7 days.

8.2 If payment remains unsuccessful after 7 days, your account will be placed in a **grace period** of 3 additional days during which the Service remains accessible.

8.3 After the grace period, your account will be **suspended** and access to the Service will be restricted to data export functionality only.

8.4 If payment is not received within 30 days of suspension, your account may be **terminated** and your data may be scheduled for deletion per our data retention policy.

8.5 You will be notified by email at each stage of the delinquency process.

## 9. Late Fees (Platform-Level)

9.1 When using Estatio's invoicing features to bill your own clients (property owners, tenants), you may configure late fees within the Platform.

9.2 Late fees applied through the Platform are your responsibility to administer and collect. Estatio facilitates the calculation and tracking of late fees but is not a collection agent.

9.3 You are responsible for ensuring late fees comply with applicable state and local laws, including maximum late fee amounts and grace period requirements.

## 10. Refund Policy

10.1 **Monthly Subscriptions:** Monthly subscription fees are non-refundable. Cancellation stops future charges; you retain access through the end of the paid period.

10.2 **Annual Subscriptions — New Customers:** If you cancel within 14 calendar days of your initial annual subscription purchase and have not generated more than 10 invoices, created more than 5 properties, or used AI processing on more than 10 documents, you may request a full refund.

10.3 **Annual Subscriptions — Renewals:** Annual renewal fees are non-refundable.

10.4 **Service Outages:** If the Service fails to meet a documented 99.0% monthly uptime SLA, you may request a service credit of 10% of your monthly fee per full hour of downtime exceeding the SLA. Credits are applied to future invoices and are not refunded as cash.

10.5 To request a refund or credit, contact **billing@estatio.app** with your account information and reason for the request within 30 days of the charge.

## 11. Cancellation

11.1 You may cancel your subscription at any time via Account Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription, or by emailing **billing@estatio.app**.

11.2 Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You retain full access through that date.

11.3 Upon cancellation, you have 30 days to export your data. After 30 days, data may be deleted per our retention policy.

11.4 Re-subscribing after cancellation may require a new setup fee and may result in data loss if re-subscribing after the 30-day data retention window.

## 12. Dispute of Charges

12.1 If you believe you have been charged incorrectly, contact **billing@estatio.app** within 60 days of the charge. We will investigate and respond within 10 business days.

12.2 Initiating a chargeback or payment dispute without first contacting us may result in immediate account suspension.

## 13. Enterprise and Custom Plans

13.1 Enterprise agreements with custom pricing, SLAs, and payment terms are available. Contact **sales@estatio.app**.

13.2 Enterprise agreements are governed by the mutually signed order form and supplemental terms, which take precedence over these standard billing terms where they conflict.
