**Estatio Inc.**
**Effective Date:** March 29, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Estatio Inc. ("Estatio," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use the Estatio property management platform ("Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, profile photo (from Google OAuth), phone number, and role within your organization.
- **Property Data:** Property addresses, ownership records, lease terms, rent amounts, and related business data.
- **Financial Data:** Invoice amounts, payment records, accounting entries, bank account details (for vendor payments), and expense records.
- **Maintenance Records:** Work order descriptions, photographs, vendor information, and maintenance history.
- **Documents:** Leases, invoices, insurance certificates, inspection reports, and other documents you upload.
- **Communications:** Support tickets, feedback, and communications with our team.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent, clicks, and navigation patterns.
- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
- **Log Data:** Server logs, error reports, and performance metrics.
- **Cookies and Tracking:** Session cookies, authentication tokens, and analytics cookies (see Section 7).

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Google OAuth:** When you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account email, name, and profile picture as authorized by you.
- **Google Cloud Platform:** Infrastructure metadata related to service operations.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

3.1 Provide, operate, and maintain the Service.

3.2 Authenticate your identity and manage your account.

3.3 Process financial transactions, generate invoices, and maintain accounting records.

3.4 Power AI-driven features including document classification, natural language search, maintenance predictions, and insight generation.

3.5 Send transactional emails such as invoice notifications, payment confirmations, and maintenance alerts.

3.6 Send service-related communications, including updates, security alerts, and support messages.

3.7 Analyze usage patterns to improve the Service.

3.8 Comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

3.9 Detect, prevent, and address fraud, security incidents, and technical issues.

We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties.

## 4. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, we process your data under the following legal bases:

- **Contract Performance:** Processing necessary to provide the Service you have subscribed to.
- **Legitimate Interests:** Analytics, security, fraud prevention, and service improvement.
- **Legal Obligation:** Compliance with applicable law.
- **Consent:** Where we have obtained your explicit consent (e.g., marketing emails).

## 6. Data Storage and Security

### 6.1 Storage Location

All data is stored on Google Cloud Platform infrastructure in the United States (region: us-central1). Documents are stored in Google Cloud Storage. Database records are stored in MongoDB Atlas.

### 6.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- TLS 1.3 encryption in transit
- AES-256 encryption at rest for documents and sensitive data
- Role-based access control (RBAC) with least-privilege principles
- JWT-based authentication with short-lived tokens
- Google Secret Manager for secrets management
- Regular security audits and vulnerability scanning

### 6.3 Data Retention

We retain your data for the duration of your subscription plus 90 days to allow for data export. Financial records may be retained for up to 7 years to comply with legal requirements. You may request deletion of your data subject to legal retention obligations.

## 7. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

- **Essential Cookies:** Required for authentication and core Service functionality. Cannot be disabled.
- **Analytics Cookies:** Used to understand how users interact with the Service (e.g., via aggregated usage metrics).
- **Preference Cookies:** To remember your settings and preferences.

You may control non-essential cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect Service functionality.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

8.1 **Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

8.2 **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

8.3 **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements).

8.4 **Portability:** Request a machine-readable copy of your data.

8.5 **Objection:** Object to certain types of processing.

8.6 **Withdraw Consent:** Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time.

8.7 **California Residents (CCPA):** You have the right to know, delete, and opt-out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To submit a CCPA request, email **privacy@estatio.app**.

To exercise your rights, contact **privacy@estatio.app**. We will respond within 30 days.
